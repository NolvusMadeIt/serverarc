
# ServerArc Privacy Policy

_Last updated: April 23, 2026_

This Privacy Policy explains how ServerArc handles information when you use the app, website pages, documentation, and related services.

## 1. Information We May Collect

Depending on how ServerArc is used, we may collect or process:

### A. Discord Account and Server Data

-   Discord user ID
-   Discord username and profile information made available through OAuth
-   server IDs
-   server names
-   roles, channels, categories, and permission structures
-   installation and application metadata needed to connect and operate the tool

### B. Local Backup and Configuration Data

-   backup files you create
-   server configuration snapshots
-   validation summaries
-   local preferences or settings

### C. Technical Data

-   basic diagnostic or error information
-   app version
-   local environment or system information needed for troubleshooting

## 2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

-   authenticate you with Discord
-   import and display Discord server structure
-   help you review, validate, and apply changes
-   create and restore backups
-   improve app reliability, safety, and usability
-   troubleshoot errors and prevent abuse

## 3. Local-First Handling

ServerArc is designed to be local-first where possible.

Backups and configuration exports may be stored on your device. You are responsible for controlling access to your local machine and files.

## 4. AI Processing

If AI-assisted guidance or validation is enabled, selected configuration data may be sent to a third-party AI provider for analysis, suggestions, or summaries.

That may include:

-   role names
-   channel names
-   permission structures
-   pending changes
-   server organization details

Sensitive tokens, secrets, and credentials should not be sent unless strictly required and intentionally configured by you.

## 5. Third-Party Services

ServerArc may interact with third-party services such as:

-   Discord
-   hosting providers
-   analytics providers, if added later
-   AI providers, if enabled

Those third parties may process information under their own privacy policies.

## 6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share data only:

-   with service providers needed to operate the app,
-   when required by law,
-   to protect rights, safety, and security,
-   or when you explicitly direct the app to connect to a third-party service.

## 7. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as reasonably necessary for operation, troubleshooting, legal compliance, and legitimate business needs.

Locally stored backups remain under your control until you delete them.

## 8. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information, but no system can guarantee absolute security.

You are responsible for:

-   protecting your device,
-   safeguarding any Discord application credentials,
-   and securing any exported backups or config files.

## 9. Children’s Privacy

ServerArc is not intended for children under 13, or under the minimum age required in your jurisdiction.

## 10. Your Choices

You may choose to:

-   disconnect Discord access
-   stop using the app
-   delete local backups
-   remove stored local data
-   revoke permissions from Discord

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted with a revised effective date.
